Cater to Your Audience With UX

User experience is critical to making your audience feel your site, services, or products are for them. Allison will use case studies to show why UX matters and how it conveys respect for your customers. Then she'll deliver tactics and advice she learned while working on MailChimp's redesign.

About

Allison Urban is a Web Analyst / UX Designer at MailChimp and owner of Lunging Bull, a tiny web studio specializing in design and development for early-stage startups. She’s also an avid reader, fiction writer and newly minted scuba diver. She loves books, the Internet, mysterious landscapes, and seeds for stories. She collects business models and creative war stories. She has a BFA in Digital and Experimental Media Art from the University of Washington. Her work has been featured in .Net Magazine, Dezeen Design and Architecture Magazine, SCRIPT Journal and MTVu.
